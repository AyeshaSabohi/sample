CHICKEN POPCORN

INGREDIENTS

  • Chicken
  • Ginger and Garlic paste
  • White pepper powder
  • Red chilli powder
  • Salt
  • Chicken powder
  • All purpose flour
  • Oil

RECIPE

  1. Marinate chicken in all spices for 2 to 3 three hours
  2. Coat in all purpose flour
  3. Dip in cold water
  4. Then again coat in all purpose flour
  5. Then deep fry in medium hot oil
  6. Now serve it and enjoy with ketchup
